About This Portal

  • Digitally manage all aspects of court cases, from filing to verdict.
  • Maintain secure and organized records for courts, judges, lawyers, and all parties involved.
  • Provide transparency and accessibility to the judicial process.
  • Support the vision of Digital India and e-Courts initiatives.

Key Features

  • User-friendly dashboard for court staff and legal professionals.
  • Advanced search and filtering for cases and parties.
  • Role-based access for security and privacy.
  • Integrated case status tracking and verdict management.
  • Support for District, High, and Supreme Courts of India.

For Indian Judiciary

This platform is tailored for Indian courts, ensuring compliance with local legal requirements and supporting multiple court types (District, High, Supreme). It is intended for use by court staff, legal professionals, and authorized parties.
